About

After spending 4 years in Dunedin, New Zealand, I graduated with a Bachelors of Pharmacy degree. This has made me somewhat proficient (but currently rusty) in all things health-science and also gifted me my most important skill. I learnt how to learn well.
I then proceeded back to my home country, Malaysia. I pursued working in my family's business where I learnt many skills. This includes marketing, human resources, business management and sales.
Due to COVID lock-down, I found myself in a bad mental state. My family insisted I stopped working. From there, I learnt a lot about managing my mental health and the importance of it.
I have since spent my days learning about web development. I have found something that piques my interest that also teaches me strong self-discipline. I am self-taught and believe that learning is a life-long process.
My current arsenals currently include Vanilla Javascript, HTML and CSS.
